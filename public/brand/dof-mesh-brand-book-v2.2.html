02 — Construction Story
A logo born from
human × machine collaboration.
The DOF-MESH logo is the result of an unprecedented creative process: a deep, iterative collaboration between Juan Carlos Quiceno (@Cyber_paisa) and Claude (Anthropic's AI) over multiple sessions of research, design, validation, and refinement. This is documented history — not mythology.
Session 1 — Research
9 AI systems consulted (DeepSeek, Kimi, Gemini, GPT, Groq, Mistral, Perplexity, GML5, Claude). Books researched: Bokhua, Draplin, Müller-Brockmann, Kenya Hara. Decision: solid fill, black & white, no color.
Session 2 — Concept
Abandoned the letterform "D" — it activated architectural associations in the brain. Confirmed by Adobe, Ramotion, 12+ sources. Problem: every D variant read as a building, door, or window section.
Session 3 — Discovery
Three new concepts tested: // (double slash), |> (pipe operator), ❯❮ (facing chevrons). Feedback from Juan: chevrons stood out most but were too thin. The gap between them was identified as the MA (meaningful void).
Session 4 — Refinement
Applied flat apex (not sharp point) — Draplin principle: thick lines. Applied 12 optical corrections. Applied neuroscience: DMN remote associativity principle. Haring test passed (200ms recognition). Score: 8.5/10.
Session 5 — Fibonacci
Discovery: the arm depth was already 34 (Fibonacci), the margin was already 8 (Fibonacci). With 1-unit adjustments: full chain F₆→F₇→F₈→F₉ verified. φ = 1.618 in every key ratio. Score: 9/10.
What makes this unique
This logo has a documented construction history spanning 5+ research sessions, 40+ validated sources, 6 skill versions, and real-time critique and refinement by a human expert in blockchain/AI governance.
Most logos are designed by one person or agency in isolation. This one was built with the combined knowledge of every major design book, paper, and researcher in the field — distilled through iterative human feedback.

05 — Color System
Monochrome as a statement.
One accent, used with restraint.
DOF-MESH operates in black and white. Color is not decoration — it is information. The green accent (#00CC55) exists only to mark verified state, active systems, and correct outputs. It is never used decoratively.
Primary Palette
Black #000000 Primary background
White #FFFFFF Logo · Primary text
Off-white #F3F2EF Light bg · Documents
Verification #00CC55 Gate · Active · Proven
Functional Colors — UI only
Surface #1A1A1A Cards · Panels
Border rgba(255,255,255,.08) Dividers · Lines
Muted text rgba(255,255,255,.35) Secondary text
Error #CC3300 Blocked · Failed
Color Usage Rules
✓ Correct use of #00CC55
System status indicators ("All systems operational"), verified state in UI, success/proof confirmations, technical documentation callouts, code highlighting for approved paths.
✗ Never use #00CC55 for
Decorative elements, backgrounds, button fills, hero sections, or the logo itself. The green is a signal — not a style. Overusing it destroys its meaning.

07 — Do's & Don'ts
The logo is a contract.
Respect it.
Misuse of the DOF-MESH mark undermines the trust and precision the brand represents. These are non-negotiable rules, not suggestions.
✓ White on black — primary Always use on pure #000 background
✗ Color logo The mark is always B&W. No exceptions.
✓ Black on off-white — secondary Only on #F3F2EF or pure white
✗ Low contrast Never use on colored or gradient backgrounds
✗ Stretched or distorted Always maintain aspect ratio 1:1
✗ Rotated The mark is always upright. 0° only.
✗ Text inside the mark Never place type inside the chevron gap
✗ Effects or shadows No drop shadows, glows, or blur

08 — Voice, Tone & Motion
Precise. Direct.
Mathematically honest.
Brand Voice

Technical

We speak in precise, specific language. We name protocols, cite verifiable numbers, reference actual implementations.

Confident

We don't qualify our claims with "might" or "could". Either it's proven or it isn't. We say what we know.

Minimal

No fluff, no filler. Every sentence earns its space. Shorter is always better. We edit ruthlessly.

Honest

We admit limitations. We publish 0% FPR results. We show the score. Trust comes from transparency, not marketing.
Voice in Practice
✗ Never say this
"Our AI solutions leverage cutting-edge blockchain technology to enhance security posture and deliver next-generation governance."
✓ Say this instead
"DOF intercepts agent decisions pre-execution and verifies them with Z3 SMT proofs. Every verification is recorded on Avalanche C-Chain. 0% false positive rate, documented."
